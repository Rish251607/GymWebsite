Divine Fitness: Services

Divine Fitness has a state-of-the-art gym equipped with top-of-the-line equipment. The gym is spacious, clean, and well-maintained, providing a comfortable environment for a workout. chest:

The chest is one of the major muscle groups in the upper body, and a strong chest is essential for overall upper body strength. The chest muscles can be targeted through exercises such as bench press, incline press, decline press, chest fly, and push-ups. These exercises can be performed using free weights such as dumbbells and barbells or weight machines that are specifically designed to target the chest muscles.

Back:

The back is another major muscle group in the upper body, and a strong back is essential for good posture and overall upper body strength. The back muscles can be targeted through exercises such as lat pulldowns, seated rows, bent-over rows, and pull-ups. These exercises can be performed using free weights or weight machines that are specifically designed to target the back muscles.

Biceps:

The biceps are the muscles on the front of the upper arm and are essential for many upper body movements. The biceps can be targeted through exercises such as bicep curls, hammer curls, and preacher curls. These exercises can be performed using free weights such as dumbbells and barbells or weight machines that are specifically designed to target the biceps.

Triceps:

The triceps are the muscles on the back of the upper arm and are essential for pushing movements. The triceps can be targeted through exercises such as tricep extensions, dips, and close-grip bench press. These exercises can be performed using free weights or weight machines that are specifically designed to target the triceps.

Shoulder:

The shoulders are another major muscle group in the upper body and are essential for many upper body movements. The shoulder muscles can be targeted through exercises such as shoulder press, lateral raise, front raise, and rear delt fly. These exercises can be performed using free weights such as dumbbells and barbells or weight machines that are specifically designed to target the shoulder muscles.

Legs:

The legs are the largest muscle group in the body and are essential for overall lower body strength. The leg muscles can be targeted through exercises such as squats, lunges, leg press, leg extensions, and leg curls. These exercises can be performed using free weights or weight machines that are specifically designed to target the leg muscles.

Abs:

The abdominal muscles are located in the midsection of the body and are essential for core strength and stability. The abs can be targeted through exercises such as crunches, sit-ups, planks, and leg raises. These exercises can be performed without any equipment or using equipment such as medicine balls or stability balls
